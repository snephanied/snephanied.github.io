About Me

Here is a general overview of the important stuff.

I was born on September 13th, 1996.

I was born in San Jose, California. Shortly after, my family moved to Riverbank, California, where I was raised. In 2012, we moved to Morgan Hill, California. This is where I presently reside and where I graduated high school.

Shorlty after graduating high school, I started college. I graduated college in 2016 with an Associates degree in Animal Sciences. I used this degree to work as a registered veterinary technician. I worked at an emergency and specialty clinic overnight. After 5 years of doing emergency medicine, I decided to switch my pace and changed to a general practice.

Shortly after getting my new job, I was in a longboarding accident.

  • I broke my left elbow.
  • I broke my right wrist.
  • I needed two surgeries on each.

Unfortunately, I then lost my job. Thats when I started getting into Web Development. Which brings me to where I am today. Currently working at Best Buy, and going to school to be a Web Developer.
