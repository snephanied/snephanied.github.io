Socials

In all honesty, I used to be completed addicted to social media. I cared what I posted, because I wanted people to view me a certain way. I curated a perfect algorithm for posting that got the most likes that I could. I obsessed over followers, view counts, likes. Just a bunch of numbers. But then, something changed. I began using social media way less. I post whatever I want and quite honestly I dont care what other people think of that. I'm not on my phone as much, I'm not obsessing over who liked what, and who was following who. I no longer was obsessed with making people only see the best parts of me. I still have the social media accounts, just to keep up with the family and friends I have on the apps, but my usage has certainly gone down. And I feel very happy about that.
