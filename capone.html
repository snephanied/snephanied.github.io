Capone

My most recent addition to the family. He was a stray brought into my work at the age of 1 day old. He still had his umbilical cord attached. I had to bottle feed him every two hours for the first while of his life. He was supposed to be just a foster, but I quickly fell in love. He has been with me for almost 3 years now.
