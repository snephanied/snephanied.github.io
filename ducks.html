Ducks

Banquet and Hop. My lovely ducks. I found Banquet as a one day old chick crossing the street - yes, just like I found Jake. I took him home and then I realized that ducks need other ducks to feel safe. So thats when I adopted Hop. They are my favorite thing to just sit and watch and listen to them quack.

The Friend

After researching and seeing what the best option would be for Banquet, I quickly learned that ducks needed friends. So I went to the local farm supply store and picked up Hop. She is an egg layer, and currently is sitting on about 21 eggs.
